## Additional Notes for all TikToks

### Production Tips:
1. **Authenticity Over Perfection** — TikTok rewards realness. Don't over-produce.
2. **Hook in First 3 Seconds** — Most viewers decide in the first 3 secs whether to keep watching.
3. **Text Overlays** — Make every video watchable without sound (many watch on mute).
4. **Trending Audio** — Use trending sounds / music from TikTok's library for algorithmic boost.
5. **Call-to-Actions** — Always end with "Download," "Tag someone," or "Link in bio."
6. **Emojis & Colors** — Use gold (#C9963C) and dark tones. Emojis make engagement better.

### Best Posting Times (Germany):
- **Weekdays:** 6-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-10 PM
- **Weekends:** 10 AM-1 PM, 6-10 PM
- Post consistently at the same times

### Engagement Boosters:
- **Respond to Comments** within the first hour (algorithm boost)
- **Pin Best Comments** to encourage more comments
- **Duet & Stitch** popular creator videos (with relevance)
- **Go Live** occasionally to build community
- **Collaborate** with health/wellness creators

### Metrics to Track:
- Views
- Completion Rate (how far people watch)
- Engagement Rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-Through Rate (to App/Bio Link)
- Download Conversions (if trackable via UTM)

### Copy Formula for TikToks:
1. **Hook:** Problem or curiosity (first 3 sec)
2. **Solution:** Show AlltagsEngel's answer (middle)
3. **Proof:** Social proof, testimonial, or benefit (before end)
4. **CTA:** Download, tag, or action (last 3-5 sec)
