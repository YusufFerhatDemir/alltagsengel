## Advanced TikTok Algorithm Hacks:

### Video Retention Optimization:
- First 3 seconds: HOOK (max drop-off point)
- Every 5 seconds: Visual change/Cut
- Average watch time: 80%+ of video (goal!)
- Rewatch rate: Track über TikTok Analytics

### Algorithm Signals zu Maximize:
1. **Watch Time:** Longer average watch = Better distribution
2. **Completion Rate:** % who finish video
3. **Engagement:** Likes, Comments, Shares, Saves
4. **Shares:** Most important signal! (> Likes)
5. **Comment Quality:** Thoughtful comments > emoji spam

### Tactics:
- Create "pause moments" (text overlay that makes people stop)
- End with question (encourages comments)
- Use "Duet" & "Stitch" features (other creators will engage)
- Trending sounds (algorithm loves this)
- Optimal video length: 21-34 seconds (our videos are perfect!)
