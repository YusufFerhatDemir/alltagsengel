## TikTok Strategy Notes:

**Posting Schedule:**
- Monday: 19:00 (Day-in-the-life — highest engagement for aspirational content)
- Wednesday: 17:00 (Edutainment — mid-week engagement peak)
- Thursday: 20:00 (Trust-building — evening wind-down when people think about life choices)

**Hook Strategy:**
- First 3 seconds MUST stop the scroll
- Use questions, curiosity, or emotional moments
- TikTok's algorithm rewards immediate engagement

**Audio Strategy:**
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Mix trending + timeless audio
- Audio drives algorithm on TikTok (50% of success)
- Always check trending sounds in your region

**Engagement Rules:**
- Respond to EVERY comment within 1 hour
- Pin supportive comments to top
- Create duets/stitches from user content
- Engage with other AlltagsEngel-adjacent creators

**Optimal Content Mix (for TikTok algorithm):**
- 40% Value/Educational (like the edutainment video)
- 40% Emotional/Inspirational (like day-in-the-life)
- 20% Transactional/CTA (like trust-building, but lighter)

**Visual Consistency:**
- Dark backgrounds (#1A1612)
- Gold accents (#C9963C)
- Quick cuts, snappy transitions
- Real people, authentic moments
- Professional but not corporate
- Warm color grading

**Text Overlay Best Practices:**
- Keep text minimal, visual-heavy
- Use modern, sans-serif fonts
- High contrast (white or Gold on dark)
- Include timestamps/relatable markers
- Emoji use is acceptable (sparingly)

**Performance Metrics to Track:**
- View count (target: 10k+ per video)
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Completion rate (watch time)
- Click-through to bio
- Follower growth rate (weekly)

**Content Calendar:**
- Post consistently Mon/Wed/Thu
- Week 2 focuses on "Unsere Engel" theme
- Shift focus weekly (next week could be: Kunde stories, Feature deep-dive, Community highlights)

**Do's & Don'ts:**

✅ DO:
- Show real people, real moments
- Be authentic & warm
- Educate (edutainment works on TikTok)
- Build community, not broadcast
- Use captions that encourage comments
- Respond quickly to comments

❌ DON'T:
- Over-edit (TikTok likes "raw" vibes)
- Use overly corporate language
- Ignore comments
- Post at random times
- Use stock footage for emotional moments
- Forget the CTA to bio link
